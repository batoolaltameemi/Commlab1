About the Video

The inspiration behind the video was to create a captivating and heartfelt portrayal of university life at NYU Abu Dhabi. The goal was to capture the essence of this unique academic experience, highlighting the challenges, personal growth, and diverse community that make it special. The video aimed to resonate with both students and the wider audience by conveying the vibrancy and complexity of university life. The story behind the poem within the video is deeply personal and reflective. It serves as a poetic narrative of the narrator's own journey through university life. Each stanza represents a significant moment or emotion experienced during this transformative period. The poem is a creative expression of the narrator's exploration, self-discovery, and growth as they navigate the challenges and opportunities that come with higher education. By weaving this original poem into the video, the creators aimed to add depth and emotion to the visual storytelling. The poem becomes the emotional heartbeat of the video, allowing viewers to connect on a deeper level with the narrator's personal experiences and reflections on their university journey. It serves as a powerful and relatable element that enhances the overall narrative of the video.

About Batool


Artist and Art enthusiast

Batool Al Tameemi, born in 2004 and based in the UAE, is an artist whose passion encompasses a diverse range of artistic techniques. Currently pursuing a major in Art and Art History with a dedicated focus on Interactive Media at New York University, her artistic endeavors extend across canvases, murals, and various mediums, delving into the depths of human emotions and societal narratives. Batool's involvement in the UAE's art scene is multifaceted. Beyond her studies, she contributes as an art assistant at Sharjah Children's Centers, where she conducted over 30 art workshops that have reached and enriched more than 1000 young artists. Batool has garnered recognition through local art competitions, including the prestigious Sheikh Sultan Award and the Qasr al Hosn's Eyes of Youth contest. Batool's work has adorned multiple galleries and exhibitions also the Emirates Embassy in Riyadh. In addition to her studies and artistic engagements, Batool takes on the role of a Graphic Designer Intern at New York University Abu Dhabi's Department of Community Outreach and Student Affairs, further showcasing her commitment to creativity and community involvement.
